Smart Poultry House Monitoring System

This system keeps a constant eye on your poultry house — 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. Sensors inside the house measure temperature, humidity, light levels, and feed weight in real time. All readings appear instantly on this dashboard so you can check on your flock from anywhere — at the farm, at home, or on your phone.

📊 What the Dashboard Shows

  • 🌡
    Temperature
    Ideal: 18 °C – 32 °C (adjust for bird age)
    Shows the current air temperature inside the house. Young chicks need higher temperatures (around 32 °C at day one, dropping 2–3 °C each week).
  • 💧
    Humidity
    Ideal: 50 % – 70 %
    Measures moisture in the air. Too low dries out the litter and irritates airways; too high promotes bacterial growth and wet litter problems.
  • 💡
    Light Level
    Ideal: varies by growth stage
    Reports brightness inside the house as a percentage. Lighting affects bird activity, feed intake, and growth. The house LED switches on automatically when it gets dark.
  • 🌾
    Feed Level
    Refill when level drops below your set threshold
    Weighs the feed in the hopper in grams. When feed runs low you will receive a warning alert so you can refill before birds go without.

🔔 Understanding Alerts

The system watches your sensor readings continuously and notifies you the moment something goes outside the safe range you have set.

  • Critical (Red) — requires immediate action. Example: temperature too high or too low. An alarm sound will play and the alert is logged so you can track when it happened.
  • Warning (Yellow) — conditions are moving outside the safe zone. Example: humidity rising or feed getting low. Act soon to prevent it becoming critical.
  • Info (Blue) — a general update, no action needed. Example: the house light was turned on or off automatically.
💡 Tip: Go to Settings to adjust the minimum and maximum values for each sensor. Set thresholds that match your bird age and breed for the most relevant alerts.

❓ Common Questions

  • The dashboard shows "--" instead of a number. What does that mean?
    The system is waiting for a reading from the sensor hardware. Check that the equipment in the poultry house is powered on and connected to Wi-Fi. Readings should appear within 30 seconds.
  • I am getting too many alerts. How do I reduce them?
    Go to Settings and widen the min/max range for the sensor that is triggering alerts. For example, if temperature warnings fire too often, raise the maximum or lower the minimum slightly.
  • Can I use this on my phone?
    Yes. The dashboard is fully responsive and works on any smartphone or tablet browser. Simply open the same web address you use on your computer.
  • How often does the data update?
    Sensor readings are sent from the hardware to the cloud every 10 seconds, so the dashboard is always close to real time.
  • How do I switch between dark and light mode?
    Use the moon/sun toggle in the top-right corner of any page. Your preference is remembered across sessions.
